Welcome to Weathery!


Weathery is a simple, intuitive weather application designed to provide accurate and real-time weather information for any city around the world. With Weathery, you can quickly access:

Current Temperature : Stay updated with the latest temperature readings in your city.
Humidity Levels : Get a sense of the moisture content in the air.
Wind Speed and Direction : Know how fast and in which direction the wind is blowing.
Feels Like Temperature : Get an estimate of how the weather actually feels outside.
Sunrise and Sunset Times : Check when the sun will rise and set, wherever you are.

Whether you're planning your day, preparing for a trip, or simply curious about the weather in a different city,Weathery is here to provide you with all the essential weather details in a clean and easy-to-understand format.
